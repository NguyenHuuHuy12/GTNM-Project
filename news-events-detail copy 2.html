Series of outstanding events of Vietnam Book and Reading Culture Day 2024

TPO - In order to implement activities of the 3rd Vietnam Book and Reading Culture Day in 2024, the Ministry of Information and Communications coordinates with ministries, branches and localities to organize a series of commemorative activities nationwide. Accordingly, Vietnam Book and Reading Culture Day 2024 is organized with the messages: "Good books need you to read", "Precious books for you", "Give good books - Buy real books", "Good books: Reading eyes". - Earphone".

Activities to celebrate Vietnam Book and Reading Culture Day 2024 will be held from April 15 to May 1, including the opening ceremony of the 3rd Vietnam Book and Reading Culture Day 2024, a welcome book fair , the launching ceremony of the first Youth Reading Day, launching competitions to learn about books associated with training, scientific research, and cultural activities of agencies and units...

Hanoi People's Committee coordinated with the Ministry of Information and Communications to organize the 3rd Vietnam Book and Reading Culture Day. The opening ceremony was held on April 17 at the Temple of Literature - Quoc Tu Giam relic. The opening program was broadcast live on Hanoi Radio and Television Station.

The Book Fair to celebrate the 3rd Vietnam Book and Reading Culture Day 2024 in Hanoi takes place in the Ho Van area (Temple of Literature - Quoc Tu Giam), including a space to introduce and promote books by publishers. , publishing businesses, technology businesses.

It is expected that 60 units will participate, with about 40,000 books along with incentives and discounts to spread reading culture and encourage reading habits. The book fair is scheduled to take place from April 17-21.

The Temple of Literature - Quoc Tu Giam Relics Area on this occasion is decorated with designs on the theme of books. In the path area, models of 6 National History books of Vietnam through the ages are displayed.

The Book Fair to celebrate the 3rd Vietnam Book and Reading Culture Day 2024 in Hanoi takes place in the Ho Van area (Temple of Literature - Quoc Tu Giam).

At the entrance area to Khue Van Cac, light technology was demonstrated and introduced to the Temple of Literature - Quoc Tu Giam. Bai Duong's courtyard area is a space to introduce electronic books and display some typical books. In the main stage area, there are also models of two sets of books: Collection of Vietnamese Literature of the 20th Century and Collection of Thang Long Hanoi.

Book introduction activities, author and work exchanges, art programs, and discussions will take place at the water pavilion area at Ho Van. In addition, many activities in Hanoi city are also taking place to respond and celebrate Vietnamese Book and Reading Culture Day, especially at Hanoi Book Street.

The launching ceremony of the first Youth Reading Day was held on April 19 at the Posts and Telecommunications Institute of Technology.

On this occasion, the Ministry of Information and Communications coordinated with the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union Central Committee and the Vietnam Publishing Association to implement a plan to organize the launching ceremony of the first Youth Reading Day on April 19 at the Postal Institute of Technology. Main Telecommunications.

The launching ceremony aims to widely deploy the activities of Vietnam Book Day and Reading Culture to each subject and area, especially young people, in order to spread the value of books and reading culture to young people. thereby building a foundation for developing reading culture in the community.
