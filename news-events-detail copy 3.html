OVERVIEW OF THE "ONWARD - MORE THAN READING" CONTEST: TOTAL PRIZE VALUE UP TO OVER 50,000,000 VND

Are you ready to explore your creative potential, conquer challenges and win extremely attractive rewards?

- Contest website: https://store.alphabooks.vn/alpha20 (section 6)

- Register to participate at: https://forms.gle/2Wk16mwxBgkrby8d9

- Time to receive entries: January 5, 2025 - February 5, 2025

- Award announcement: February 8, 2025

“Onward - More than reading” – a creative playground dedicated to book-loving souls has officially opened to receive entries! This is where you can spread your love for books, express your personality, inspire, and leave your own mark.

Participation rules are extremely simple:
Just choose a favorite book from Alpha Books and do 1 of the following 2 challenges:
- Challenge 1: “Books come to life”
Create content based on stories, situations, excerpts or lessons from your favorite books. You can use art forms such as acting, cosplay, staging situations, painting, storytelling,...
- Challenge 2: “Books in me”
Share your thoughts or review about your favorite book from Alpha Books.

So what are you waiting for?

• The total prize value is up to more than 50 million VND.
• Opportunity to connect with the book-loving community across the country
