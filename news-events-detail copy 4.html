Many meaningful activities at Vietnam Book and Reading Culture Day

Book and Reading Culture Day in Hanoi not only honors those who write and make books, but more deeply, honors readers and revives reading culture.

A series of events organized by Hoan Kiem District People's Committee in coordination with publishers, bookstores, related units and individuals, in response to the third Vietnam Book and Reading Culture Day (April 21, 2024), Celebrating the 7th anniversary of the establishment of Hanoi Book Street (May 1, 2017 - May 1, 2024).

April 17, at the Temple of Literature - Quoc Tu Giam (Hanoi), the Ministry of Information and Communications coordinated with the Central Propaganda Committee, the Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism, and the Hanoi People's Committee to organize an organization. held the opening ceremony of the third Vietnam Book and Reading Culture Day 2024.

The event was organized to promote the good traditional values ​​of the nation, clearly realizing the role and importance of books and reading in the development of social life.

The third Vietnam Book and Reading Culture Day in Hanoi takes place from April 17 to May 1 with many rich and diverse activities and events to honor books and promote the development of reading culture.

Some notable events include: Exhibition and Book Fair to celebrate the third Vietnam Day of Books and Reading Culture; Online exhibition and book fair serving Vietnamese people abroad; Launching ceremony of Reading Day among Youth... along with many activities to celebrate Book Day and Reading Culture across the country.

Speaking at the opening ceremony, Minister of Information and Communications Nguyen Manh Hung emphasized that Book and Reading Culture Day not only honors those who write books and make books, but more deeply, honors readers and revives them. reading culture.

"This year, the Day of Books and Reading Culture hopes that the publishing industry will contribute to building the national knowledge system, developing a team of intellectuals, and meeting the needs of industrialization and modernization of the country. Approved chain of events, bringing a new spirit, where people are, reading culture has developed, where readers are, books are near," said Minister Nguyen Manh Hung.

At the opening ceremony, Head of the Central Propaganda Department Nguyen Trong Nghia requested relevant ministries, branches and agencies to coordinate in performing a number of tasks: Promoting propaganda, education and raising awareness in community about the meaning and importance of books and reading culture; Focus on investing, supporting, and establishing institutions for reading promotion activities in schools, agencies, and units at all levels; Strengthen and encourage the creation of books and works of high value, serving the increasingly diverse needs of readers; Promote digital transformation in publishing, printing and book distribution culture, creating new value.

Head of the Central Propaganda Department Nguyen Trong Nghia and Minister of Information and Communications visited book stalls. (Photo: Diem Hang/Vietnam+)

One of the highlights of the 3rd Vietnam Day of Books and Reading Culture is the Book Fair program held from April 17, 2024 to April 21, 2024 in the Ho Van area of ​​the relic complex. Temple of Literature - Quoc Tu Giam, with the participation of nearly 60 publishing and book distribution units across the country.

The Book Fair with many attractive programs and exchange events, diverse in genre and number of publications, promises to become a festival for readers and book lovers.

Especially at the book exhibition space, readers will admire precious books about the special national historical relic Temple of Literature - Quoc Tu Giam; photos and books about the country and people of Vietnam in modern electronic versions and direct editions. This year's Book Fair also attracted about 60 participating units, displaying and providing over 40,000 valuable books.

The response to activities to celebrate the third Vietnam Book and Reading Culture Day - 2024 in Hanoi City has contributed to developing reading culture for the people of the Capital, creating beauty in cultural life. culture - society, building elegant - civilized Hanoi people, preserving and promoting the value of Thang Long - Hanoi thousands of years of culture.

In addition to traditional activities, Vietnam Book and Reading Culture Day 2024 also combines modern organizational methods following the development trend of technology platforms. In particular, Fintech Momo Company has brought a number of solutions such as Business Pages to help publishers and book publishers put their stores on a platform with more than 30 million users. In addition, MoMo also built a community for book lovers called "Book Lovers - Review Addicts" right on the platform. This is where users can post and refer to quality book reviews of good books, connect with people with similar interests in books, thereby contributing to spreading reading culture in the digital environment. …
